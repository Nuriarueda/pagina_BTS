HISTORY

BTS also known as the Bangtan Soyeondan (방탄소년단) is a korean boy band that formed in 2010 and debuted on 13th June of 2013 with their first song called “No More Dream” under Big Hits Entertainments.

This south korean boy band is formed by RM, Jin, J-Hope, V, Jimin and Jungkook who co-writes and co-produces much of their own material.

The reason why they called Bangtan Soyeondan (BTS) is because in korean that means bulletproof Boy Scouts, they choose this name because they want to leave behind all the stereotypes and the bad comments dodging them like bullets

Each BTS member has their own rol on the group: RM is the leader of the boyband, Suga is the lead rapper, J-Hope is the main dancer , Jin is the visual of the group, dancer and vocalist, Jimin is the main vocalist, Jungkook like Jimin he is the main vocalist, he is a good dancer and composer and V is vocalist, dancer, composer, visual and actor.
